Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.
It has a diverse climate, a protective atmosphere, and vast oceans covering 70% of its surface.
Earth's magnetic field and ozone layer shield it from harmful solar radiation.

Interesting Facts:
- Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System.
- It has one moon, known simply as "the Moon."
- The Earth's atmosphere contains 21% oxygen, essential for life.
