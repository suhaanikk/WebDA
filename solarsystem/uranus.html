Uranus

Uranus is a unique planet that rotates on its side, making its axis nearly horizontal. It is an ice giant, with a pale blue-green color due to methane in its atmosphere.
Uranus has a faint ring system and 27 known moons.

Interesting Facts:
- A day on Uranus is 17 hours, but a year is 84 Earth years.
- Uranus was the first planet discovered with a telescope in 1781.
- Its moons are named after characters from works by Shakespeare and Alexander Pope.
