Mars

Mars, the "Red Planet," is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is home to the tallest volcano (Olympus Mons) and the largest canyon (Valles Marineris) in the Solar System.
Mars has a thin atmosphere, mostly carbon dioxide, and temperatures are typically very cold.

Interesting Facts:
- Mars has the largest dust storms in the Solar System, lasting for months and covering the entire planet.
- Evidence suggests that liquid water once flowed on Mars.
- Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos.
