Saturn

Saturn is famous for its intricate ring system, made mostly of ice particles and rock. It is the second-largest planet and a gas giant like Jupiter.
Saturn's low density means it could float in water if a large enough ocean existed.

Interesting Facts:
- Saturn has 146 moons and moonlets, with Titan being the largest.
- Its rings are relatively young, estimated to be about 100 million years old.
- Saturn's winds can reach speeds of over 1,800 km/h (1,118 mph).
