Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun. It is a deep blue ice giant with supersonic winds that reach up to 2,100 km/h (1,300 mph).
Neptune has a dynamic atmosphere with storms and dark spots similar to Jupiter's Great Red Spot.

Interesting Facts:
- A year on Neptune is 165 Earth years.
- Triton, its largest moon, orbits in the opposite direction of Neptune's rotation.
- Neptune emits more energy than it receives from the Sun due to internal heat.
